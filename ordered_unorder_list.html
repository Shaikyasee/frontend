How to do chicken fry

simplified recipe for chicken fry

INGREDIENTS

for the dough

  • Chicken pieces (500 grams)
  • Ginger-garlic paste (1 tsp)
  • Red chili powder (1 tsp)
  • Turmeric powder (¬Ω tsp)
  • Garam masala or curry powde

for the filling

  • Salt (to taste)
  • Oil (2 tbsp)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving, optional)

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add marinated chicken and cook for 8-10 mins until golden and fully cooked.
  3. Garnish with coriander leaves. Serve hot with lemon wedges on the side.Enjoy your simple and delicious chicken fryüçó
